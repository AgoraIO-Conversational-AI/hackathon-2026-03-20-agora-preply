# Lesson themes: emu-uk-en-a1-01

## Lesson arc

The arc moves from receptive to productive, from controlled to free, building confidence incrementally. By the final role-play, Oksana handles an unexpected customer request (changing juice preference) independently - a clear sign of communicative growth within the lesson.
