Preply x Agora Hackathon: AI Agents for NextGen Language Learning

Join Preply and Agora for a high-impact, 1.5-day hackathon at Preply’s Barcelona Office. We’re bringing together some of the city’s strongest engineering talent to build the next generation of AI-powered language learning using agent-based and real-time technologies.

​This is your opportunity to create solutions that help learners progress faster, understand their growth clearly, and speak with confidence in everyday situations.

​You’ll get hands-on access to technologies from Agora, OpenAI, AWS, Anam, and Thymia to prototype intelligent systems that push applied GenAI forward.

To kick off the evening, we've invited Max Hudlberger (Applied AI @ OpenAI) and Ben Weekes (Convo AI @ Agora), who will open the hackathon with a short keynote and stay for the discussion. Max will share how OpenAI's latest advancements in Codex and related tooling enable developers to build next-generation systems, while Ben will present Agora's conversational AI platform. Both Max and Ben will be around to join other mentors to answer questions, exchange ideas, and advise teams experimenting with agentic development.

​If you’re excited about building AI agents that actually change how people learn and communicate, this is your room! 🤝
