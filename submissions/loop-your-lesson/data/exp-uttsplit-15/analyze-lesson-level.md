## Gaps

### Prepositional government (verb + preposition + case) (accuracy) - exercise_focus: true
Systematic errors with verb-preposition-case combinations: ukryc od ciebie instead of ukryc przed toba (27:36), przerywac mnie instead of przerywac mi (23:34), klocic sie za glupote instead of o glupote (18:49), czekac mnie instead of czekac na mnie. This was the central teaching focus of the lesson, indicating an active learning gap.

### Limited discourse connectors (coherence) - exercise_focus: true
Relies primarily on basic connectors: i, ale, bo, no i, na przyklad. Does not spontaneously produce subordinating connectors (chociaz, mimo ze), contrastive markers (z drugiej strony, natomiast), or result markers (w zwiazku z tym, dlatego tez). This limits coherence in extended arguments.

### Gender agreement in past tense (accuracy) - exercise_focus: true
Some mixing of masculine and feminine past tense endings. While partly attributable to transcription artifacts (avg confidence 0.76), the pattern suggests incomplete automatization of gender marking in past tense verbs, a common Ukrainian-speaker error in Polish.

### Domain-specific and abstract vocabulary (range) - exercise_focus: false
Gaps when conversation moves beyond core everyday topics: needs teacher help with morela suszona (38:08), zurawina (38:21), fashion terminology during reading exercise. Uses L1 calques in unfamiliar domains: aberkosy, prekrasy, promizowac (08:07).

1. **[P1]** Continue prepositional government exercises using error-finding texts - this lesson's approach directly targets the main accuracy gap. Focus on high-frequency verb+preposition pairs: czekac na, klocic sie o, ukryc przed, przerywac komus, opowiedziec o
2. **[P2]** Introduce wider discourse markers through guided conversation: chociaz, mimo ze, z drugiej strony, poza tym, w zwiazku z tym. Model their use and encourage Vasyl to incorporate one new connector per lesson
3. **[P3]** Use Vasyl's technical interests (AI, hackathons, Preply platform) as contexts for practicing B2-level structures - this is a high-motivation domain where he can stretch his accuracy and range simultaneously

### For student
1. **[P1]** Continue prepositional government exercises using error-finding texts - this lesson's approach directly targets the main accuracy gap. Focus on high-frequency verb+preposition pairs: czekac na, klocic sie o, ukryc przed, przerywac komus, opowiedziec o
